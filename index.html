our menu MAIN COFFEE BEAN TYPES

Liberica

Flavour: Liberica beans are known for their unique fruity and floral notes, often described as having a woody or smoky taste.

Popularity: Liberica beans are less common and have a niche market compared to Arabica and Robusta.

Arabica

Flavour: Arabica beans are prized for their complex flavours, often featuring fruity, floral, and acidic notes with varying degrees of sweetness.

Popularity: Arabica beans are the most widely consumed and preferred type of coffee globally, prized for their superior taste and aroma.

Robusta

Flavour: Robusta beans are known for their strong, bold flavour with earthy and nutty notes, often exhibiting a bitter taste.

Popularity: Robusta beans are commonly used in espresso blends and instant coffees due to their strong flavour and higher caffeine content.

Excelsa

Flavour: Excelsa beans have a unique flavour profile, featuring fruity and tart notes along with a hint of spiciness and a medium body.

Popularity: Excelsa beans are less common than Arabica and Robusta but are valued for their distinctive flavour contributions in certain coffee blends.
